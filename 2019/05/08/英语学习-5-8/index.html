英语学习 - 5/8

发表于 2019-05-08 | 更新于: 2019-05-09 | 分类于 日常生活 | 阅读次数:
字数统计: 1,599 | 阅读时长 ≈ 7

Fake it until you become it.

100 个句子

先从15个开始吧，前面的后面再补

  1. Thanks to modern irrigation, crops now grow abundantly in areas where once nothing but cacti and sagebrush could live.

    多亏了现代灌溉技术, 农作物在原来只有仙人掌和荞属科植物才能生存的地方旺盛的生长

    • irrigation: noun. 灌溉; (临床医学) 冲洗法

      ①. 灌溉, irrigate( this is the verb form of irrigation) means to supply(something, such as land) with water by artificial means (such as pipes(管))

      ②. irrigation in medical is a method to clean a wound(noun. 伤口 vt. 使受伤) or a part of the body by following water.

    • crop: noun. 农作物;谷物 verb. 种植

    • cacti: noun. 仙人掌

    • sagebrush: 荞属科植物; 蒿属植物

    • abundantly: 丰富的大量的

  2. The development of mechanical timepieces spurred the search for more accurate sundials with which to regulate them.

    机械计时器的发展，促使人们寻求更精确的日晷，以便校准机械计时器。

    • mechanical: adjective. 机械的；力学的

      mechanics. 机械学

      mechaniery. 机械

      mechanize. 使…机械化

      mechanism. 机制；原理

    • timepiece: noun. 钟等各种计时器

    • spur:

      • noun. 鼓舞，刺激；马刺；山坡；
      • verb. 刺激；激励；骑马疾驰；给予刺激

    • sundial: noun. 日晷

    • regulate: 规定；调节；校准

    • accurate: 精确的

      表示精确的词语

      faithful, precise, rigid, refined; exact; pinpoint

  3. Anthropology is a science in that anthropologists use a rigorous set of methods and techniques to document observations that can be checked by others.

    人类学使一门科学，因为人类学家采用了一整套强有力的方法和技术来记录和观测结果，而这样记录下来的观测结果是供他人核查的。

    • Anthropology: 人类学
    • Anthropologist: 人类学家
    • rigorous: adjective. 严格的；严厉的；严密的；严酷的
    • document: verb. 记录 noun. 文档

  4. Fungi are important in the process of decay, which returns ingredients to the soil, enhances soil fertility, and decomposes animal debris.

    真菌在腐化过程中十分重要，而腐化过程将物质回馈于土壤，提高其肥力，并分解动物粪便。

    • Fungi: noun. 真菌；菌类；蘑菇；(Fungus的复数)

    • decay: verb and noun. 腐化；腐烂；腐朽；衰败；

      decomposition: 分解，腐烂，变质; decompose: 分解；使腐烂

    • enhance: 提高

    • soil: 土壤

    • ingredients: noun. 要素；原料；组成部分

      柯林斯

      • Ingredients are the things that are used to make something, especially all the different foods you use when you are cooking a particular dish.(特指)烹饪的原料
      • An Ingredient of a situation is one of the essential parts of it. 要素

    • fertility: noun. 多产；肥沃；土壤肥力

    • decompose: verb. 分解;使腐烂

  5. When it is struck, a tuning fork produces an almost pure tone, retaining its pitch over a long period of time.

    音叉被敲击时，产生几乎纯质的音调，其音量经久不衰

    • fork: noun. 叉 verb. 分叉

    • pure: 纯的

    • retain: 保持

    • pitch: 音量

      pitch还有很多其它意思

  6. Although pecans are most plentiful in the southeastern part of the United States, they are found as far north as Ohio and Illinois.

    虽然美洲山核桃树(pecan)集中于美国的东南部但是在北至俄亥俄州(Ohio)以及伊利诺伊州(illinois)也能看见他们

    • plentiful: 丰富的；许多的；众多的；丰饶的

      表示丰富的词语还有

      abundant, numerous, many

  7. Eliminating problems by transferring the blame to others is often called scapegoating.

    用怪责别人的方法解决问题通常被称为寻找替罪羊

    • Eliminate: vt. 消除

      Eliminate Problem 解决问题

    • scapegoating: 代罪;

  8. The chief food eaten in any country depend largely on what grows best in its climate and soil.

    一个国家的主要食物是什么大体取决于什么作物在其天气和土壤条件下生长的好

    • chief: 首要的；主要的
    • climate: 气候；天气
    • soil: 土壤

  9. Over a very large number of trials, the probability of an event’s occurring is equal to the probability that it will not occur.

    大量的实验表明，某一事件发生的概率等于它不发生的概率

    • trial: 试验；审讯，审判；努力；磨练；

  10. Most substance contract when they freeze so that the density of a substance’s solid is higher than the density of its liquid.

    大多数物质遇冷收缩，所以他们的密度在固态时高于液态

    • substance: 物质
    • contract:
      • verb. 收缩； 签合同；
      • noun. 合同；婚约
    • density: 密度

  11. The mechanism by which brain cells store memories is not clearly understood.

    大脑细胞储存记忆的机理并没有被清楚的理解

    • mechanism: 机制；机理；原理
    • store: 存储
    • cell: 细胞

  12. By the middle of the twentieth century, painters and sculptors in the United States had begun to exert a great worldwide influence over art.

    到了二十世纪中叶，美国画家和雕塑家开始在世界范围内对艺术产生重大影响

    • sculptor: 雕塑家

    • sculpture: 雕塑；雕刻；刻蚀

      engrave: 雕刻

      etch: 蚀刻

    • over: 在…上, influence over art, 对艺术产生影响

  13. In the eastern part of New Jersey lies the city of Elizabeth, a major shipping an dmanufacturing center.

    新泽西州东部坐落着伊丽莎白市，这是一个重要的航运和制造业中心.

    • manufacturing: 制造业
    • manufacture: 制造；制造业；

  14. Elizabeth Blackwell, the first woman medical doctor in the United States, founded the New York Infirmary, an institution that has always had a completely femle medical staff.

    Elizabeth Blackwell, 美国第一个女性医生，创建了纽约医院, 一个总是有完整女性医疗员工的机构

    • infirmary: 医院；养老院；

    • institution: 机构

    • staff: noun. 职员; adj.职员的; v. 雇佣职员

    • found: 创建;find过去式

      表示创建的词语

      establish

  15. Alexander Graham Bell once told his family that he would rather be remembered as a teaher of the deaf than as the invertor of the teleohone.

    Alexander Graham Bell 曾告诉家人，他更愿意让后人记住他是聋子的老师，而不是电话的发明人

单词

  • diffuse: 扩散；传播；漫射；散开

    diffuse conflict: 化解冲突

  • differential: adjective. 微分的;差别的;特异的; noun. 微分;差别;

    reduce status differentials 化解阶级差异

  • retention: noun. 保留；扣留；滞留；(记忆力) memory retention

  • cohesiveness: 凝聚力;内聚力;

    social cohesiveness 社会凝聚力

    group cohesiveness 群体凝聚力

  • obsess: 痴迷; 迷住；困扰

    柯林斯: 困扰;心神不宁; If something obsesses you or if you obsess about something, you keep thinking about it and find it difficult to think about anything else.

    slander 流言蜚语

    libel 诽谤

    scandal 丑闻

    表示痴迷,沉迷于,醉心于,全神贯注于的词语:

    be absorbed in, be hooked on, be addicted to, indulge, be lost in, be rapt in

  • overwhelm: v. 受打击(被动)；adjective. 压倒的；不知所措的；

    柯林斯: If you are overwhelmed by a feeling or event, it affects you very strongly, and you do not know how to deal with it.
