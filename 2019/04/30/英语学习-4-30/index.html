英语学习 4/30

发表于 2019-04-30 | 更新于: 2019-05-13 | 分类于 日常生活 | 阅读次数:
字数统计: 960 | 阅读时长 ≈ 4

单词

  • sever: 分离切开，cut off

  • densely: 稠密地

  • clay:粘土

  • circumstance:

    有道

    • 环境，情况；事件；境遇

  • compounds: 混合物，化合物

  • thigh：大腿

  • libel: 诽谤

  • slander: 流言

  • mechanize：自动化，机械化

  • vague: 模糊的

  • obscure: 模糊的，昏暗的，隐蔽的；使模糊不清

  • irrigation：灌溉技术

  • rigorous：严厉的；严格的；严密的；严酷的

  • comprise: 由…组成;包含

    柯林斯: If you say that something comprises or is comprised of a number of things or people, you mean it has them as its parts or members.

表示由…组成, 包含的词语还有:

consist of, contain, include, cover, constitute

  • latency: 潜在因素, 软件场景下指延迟, 如 additive latency.

  • segment:

    • noun. 部分;节;音段；段

      表示部分的词还有: section, portion, part

    • v. 分隔,(细胞)分裂

  • With respect to sth.: 关于sth.

    表示关于的词组还有: about, in regard to, relate to, in relation to, involved, concerning, in the case of, with reference to

  • finer: 出色的；好的;

    近义词: good, ok, great, cool, cracking

  • adjacency: n. 邻近；四周；邻接物；

  • proposition: 命题；提议；主题；议题；

  • catalog:

    • noun. 目录；登记；
    • v: 等级；为…编目录

  • bulk:

    • noun. 大量；大多数
    • v. 使扩大；使大量

  • enhance: 提高;加强;增加

    近义词: 表示提高，增强，加强的词语还有:

    raise, cement, boost //待核实

  • constitute: vt. 组件；构成；建立；任命

  • augment:

    • noun. 增加；增大
    • verb. 增大；增加
    • 柯林斯: To augment something means to make it larger, stronger, or more effective by adding someting to it. 增加(FORMAL)

    表示增加，增大的词语还有:

    improve, gain, accelerarte, enhance, boost, jack, mount, increase, rise, accession //待核实 来自有道词典

  • coherence: noun. 一致；连贯性；凝聚

    柯林斯: N-UNCOUNT(不可数名词), Coherence is a state or situation in which all the parts or ideas fit together well so that they form a united whole. 连贯性

  • resolution: noun. 分辨率; 决议；解决；决心;

    柯林斯:

    • N-COUNT, A resolution is a formal decision made at meeting by means of a vote. 决议

    • N-COUNT, If you make a resolution, you decide to try very hard to do something. 决心

    • N-UNCOUNT, Resolution is determination to do something or not do something. 决心

    • N-SING, The resolution of a problem or difficulty is the final solving of it. 最终解决[FORMAL]

    表示解决的词语:

    solve/solution, resovle/resolution,settle/settlement, work out, figure out,
