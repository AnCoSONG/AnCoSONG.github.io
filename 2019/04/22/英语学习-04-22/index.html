You can’t win the war against the world, if you cannot win the war against your own mind.

单词

  • conference: 研讨会

  • overgeneralize: 以偏概全；做过分概括的陈述；说话过于笼统

  • a few doors down: 离你所在的位置不远

    这个答案来源于: 这个网站

  • technician: 技师；技术员；

  • happen to：正好；碰巧

  • syllabus: 教学大纲；摘要；课程表

  • the other day：几天前；不久前某一天

  • printout: 打印的资料;打印出的东西

  • elective: 选修的；选举的；选任的；选修课程

  • depart: 离开；违反；韦氏：to change something or do something in a different way（违反）；to leave a place especially to start a journey（出发）

    • depart for: 出发
    • depart from：从…出发；离开

  • specialty: 特长；特点；专长；招牌菜；

  • fabric: 织物

  • sculpture: 雕塑

  • discount: 打折

  • composition: 作品；作曲；合成物；

  • embody

    • to be a symbol or example of something，使具体化，成为…的象征或例子: He is a leader who embodies courage == He is a very courageous leader
    • 体现,to include (something) as a part or feature: The new law embodies a revenue provision.新法律体现了收入规定

精听

TPO42 Conversation 1

这篇说实话有点惨, 2/5,好好精听一下

Listen to a conversation between a student and an art history professor.

Hi, I am Melisa. I was just a few doors down getting some help in the computer lab. My electronic files won’t open. The technician(技术人员) says it’s probably a computer virus. She’s working on it now.

Yes, from what I’ve heard lots of campus computers have been affected.What a first week Huh?

I know, anyhow, I noticed your name on the door as I was walking down the hall, thought I’d stop in and find out if you happen to have any additional copies of the class syllabus.

这句非常长，但核心意思其实就是，碰巧路过，想问syllabus(教学大纲)

The one I received in class the other day is missing a page.

我那天在课上收到的那一份，少了一张

突然觉得我应该只精听长的重点部分，尤其是听不懂的部分

I probably have a few extra printouts on hand.

我手头可能还有一些额外的复印本

Great. Oh I noticed on the syllabus, we’ll be learning about and eventually writing a paper on “The Bauhaus style of art”?

译文: 太好了，而且我注意到了课程大纲，我们将会学习，并且最终也会写一篇有关”Bauhaus艺术风格”的论文

这一句又是比较长而且有生词Bauhaus以及and eventually这一句没有听清楚

Right, but technically it doesn’t say the Bauhaus style of art.

但是严格来说,它并没有说是Bauhaus艺术风格

Ok, the Bauhaus is not really an artisitic style like cubism.

包豪斯并不像立体主义是一种艺术形式

It was a name of an art and design school in Germany in early 20 centuray.

The Bauhaus was started as an experiment in education, and one ground-breaking technique used in its teaching was that students actively participated in workshops instead of just sitting in classes.

这句话又是非常长，意识是去解释为什么说包豪斯不是艺术形式，be started as 说明这个Bauhaus最开始是作为教育实验的，后面解释了是一个怎么样的实验：是要求学生积极参与，而不仅仅是做在教室里

Interesting! I don’t have much background in order or anything.

我对这块没什么背景知识

I am an economic major and I am taking this class as an elective, decided I want to broaden my awareness, try something new.

解释为什么没有背景知识

我主修经济学，而我选这门课是作为我的选修课，希望能够拓宽我的见识，尝试学一些新的东西。

So, was the focus of Bauhaus architecture? I mean, I studied German and Bauhaus was translated into house for building.

大意就是问是不是包豪斯关注建筑，并解释了原因：学过德语，德语的Bauhaus是建筑的房屋的意思

Well, the founding director was an architect(建筑学家).

是的，这门艺术的创始人确实是建筑学家

However, he aimed to combine an incredibly broad variety of fine arts and crafts under one artisitic roof.

但是，他立志于将艺术和工艺结合在同一艺术的屋檐下，这很不可思议

As a matter of fact, when the Bauhaus first opened, it was without an architecture department for several year. But later it becomes very influencial in architecture（建筑）.

You’ll see on the syllabus you’re required to visit the Rutherford Museum(博物馆) exhibit.

The exhibit will help you see that there is no single Bauhaus style.

I think it’s refreshing that this particular exhibit departs from the standard way in which art from the Bauhaus is often presented.

说明这个展会的不同，眼前一亮之处

I think it’s a mistake to focus on a single Bauhaus artist and that person’s individual specialty

我认为，仅仅关注某一个包豪斯艺术家以及那位艺术家的个人特色是错误的

I mean the different artists from the school created different things: fabric, sculpture, furniture, graphic design, paintings, even theatrical performance.

我的意思是说，学校里不同的艺术家，会创造出不同的东西：举了一些例子：织物，雕塑，家具，平面设计，美术作品，甚至是戏剧表演

The exhibit in the Rutherford Museum units all these specialty through connecting things such as motion or the body.

卢瑟福博物馆的展览，把所有的这些东西根据 动作 身体等 不同的主题，相结合在了一起

Say, I’ve heared something about the discount nights at that museum?

啊，我听说那个博物馆有类似于打折夜这样的活动？

Weekends are full price.

周末全价

It’s typically best to go Thursday night.

通常来说周四去最好

That’s students’ discount night, 50 percent off.

那是学生打折夜，给学生打5折

However, Next Wednesday is open to the public for free.

It’s a special promotion

是一场推广
