生词

  • leak：泄露，流出
  • dump：扔掉
  • bound for: 开往
  • pirates: 海贼：someone who attacks and steals from a ship at sea. 盗版者：someone who illegally copies a product or invention without permission. verb. To illegally copy without permission.
  • gooey: wet and sticky；胶粘的； 感伤的；(韦氏：often used figuratively. a sentimental romantic comedy)
  • snarl: a situation which you can no long move or make progress( traffic snarl 交通拥堵); a twisted knot of hairs 头发缠在一起。 这个词就是表示缠在一起。经常会拟人化，用来形容 交通拥堵，或者 作动词表示form a snarl,造成纠缠的情况， the accidents snarl traffic.
  • valve：活塞，阀门的感觉； 韦氏：a mechanical device that controls the flow of liquid, gas,etc.
  • leak: v. 泄露（从一个洞里）to let something (such as a liquid or gas) in or out through a hole in a surface；公之于众：to become known to the public；n. 漏洞；
  • come across: something you find by accident, 无意发现；偶然发现
  • sealant: a substance that is put on a surface in order to protect it from air, water. 密封胶。
  • layaway: 买东西的一种方式，你先付一部分钱，之后补齐剩下的款项，当你补齐款项的时候，你就可以得到这个东西
  • sweeping: 广泛的。wide in range or amount
  • restore：恢复；还原
  • spring：春天;跳； to move or leap suddenly forward or upward; 泉水；a quick sudden leap up or forward.
  • sleigh：雪橇
  • thistle：蓟；a wild plant that has sharp points on its leaves and purple, yellow, or white flowers
  • exclaim：to say something in an enthusiastic or forceful way. 大喊，喊叫
  • sweat: 流汗；
  • pant：喘气；to breathe hard and quickly；
  • spin：旋转
  • damn：used to show that you are angry, annoyed, surprised, etc；used to show that you are angry or annoyed at a person, thing, or situation; USED FOR RELAX INFORMAL SITUATION.

DD精听

Daily Dictation 36

when the family left Vietnam by boat, bound for(开往…) Thailand, but the boat was intercepted by pirates(海贼，海盗). After years of trying, the father located his son in Thailand.

Daily Diction 37

A gooey（胶粘的） mess snarl traffic(Cause Traffic Problem, snarl 使纠缠) along the busy Pennsylvania Turnpike(The road you must pay to use). A leaking(泄露的) valve（活塞，活门）on a tanker(a ship carry oil or gas, a truck carry oil or gas) spread driveway sealant(车道密封胶？) across a 40-mile stretch of the the eastbound（东侧的） side.

Daily Dictation 38

The music is being played at a church in Germany. and well, it really lives up to its name(名副其实的). Since it started, there have been just 11 chord changes and the next one isn’t scheduled until July 2012.

Daily Dictation 39

Dozens(12, or more 可以指12，也可以指很多) of white rabbits were apparently dumped(to put somewhere in a quick and careless way, to throw away 扔掉) along the side of the road Sunday night. More than 60 bunnies（小兔子，可爱女郎） were scattered(分散、分开) on the shoulder

Daily Dictation 40

There’s a wonderful line（一句话，类似slogen，） I came across: If you always do what you’ve always done, you’ll always get what you’ve always got. It’s a brilliant line!

Daily Dictation 41

Her story has inspired others from Montana to Indiana to pay off the bills of complete stranger in these days before Christmas. Holiday shopper who’ve heard about the layaway santas say the sweeping acts of kindness have restored their faith in people.

Daily Dictation 42

He sprang to his sleigh to his team gave a whistle. And away they all flew like the down of a thistle. But I heard him exclaim as he drove out of sight（驾驶着离开视角） “Happy Christmas to all and to all a good night”.
